About Trimurti

Founded in 2010, Trimurti Facility Services has grown from a small local service provider to one of India's most trusted facility management companies. We serve over 500 clients across residential complexes, commercial buildings, hotels, hospitals, and industrial facilities.

Our experienced workforce is trained to handle different aspects of facility management including security operations, maintenance services, cleaning, and infrastructure support. We ensure that every project is handled with care, efficiency, and strict quality standards.

Modern organizations require well-managed infrastructure to ensure productivity and safety. Proper facility management helps organizations reduce operational costs, maintain hygienic workplaces, and create better environments for employees and visitors.
